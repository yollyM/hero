Childhood

Born in 29 January 1954, Koscuisko Mississippi, Oprah is a media proprietor, talk show host, producer, actress and a philanthropist. She grew up in great poverty wearing potatoe sack to a single teenage mother and during her childhood she was molested but she never got her dreams and aspirations. Oprah later moved to live with her father and she said having a strict father saved her life because it provided her with structure, guidance, books and rules. She was required to complete weekly book reports to her father, and she went without dinner until she learnt five new vocabulary word each day. Winfrey be came an excellent student, participating in debate club, drama club and student council, she won a full scholarship to Tennessee State University and that was a turning point in her life.

Career

Still in high school Oprah was invited to a White House Conference on Youth and was crowned Miss Fire Prevention by WVOL a local Nashville radio station and was hired by the station for afternoon newscasts.In University Oprah studied Journlism while still working in the radio station . Columbia Broadcasting System(CBS) offered her a job, Winfrey turned it down twice but was finally advised by her teacher that CBS was the reason people go to college , at nineteen Oprah become the first Nashville African American female co-anchor of the evening news.After graduating Winfrey blossomed she move to Balitmore and did local news updates , morning talk show, few years later American Broadcasting Company(ABS) offered Oprah A.M Chicago and changed the emphasis of a tradional women's issues to current and controversial topics, the ratings of that show grew and the show was later renamed The Oprah Winfrey Show.
